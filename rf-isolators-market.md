<p><ul><li>Coaxial Isolator</li><li>Drop-In Isolator</li><li>Surface Mount Isolator</li><li>Microstrip Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF isolators are crucial components in communication systems, ensuring signal integrity by preventing reflected signals from causing interference. The market features various types: Coaxial isolators are designed for connecting coaxial cables and are popular in laboratory and field applications. Drop-in isolators are compact and fit into existing circuits easily. Surface mount isolators provide a miniaturized option for modern electronics, while microstrip isolators are integrated into RF circuits on substrates, ideal for high-frequency applications. Each type is tailored for specific use cases.</p></p>
