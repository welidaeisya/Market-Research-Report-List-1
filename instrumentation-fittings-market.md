<p><strong>The Instrumentation Fittings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube</li><li>Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Instrumentation fittings in the market include tube and pipe types, each serving distinct applications. Tube fittings are designed for high-pressure and fluid control applications, ensuring leak-free connections in tight spaces. They are typically made from materials like stainless steel and are used in instrumentation and hydraulic systems. Pipe fittings, on the other hand, connect larger piping systems, facilitating fluid transfer in industrial settings. They are often used in water, gas, and chemical transport, prioritizing durability and flow efficiency.</p></p>
