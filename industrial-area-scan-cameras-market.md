<p><p>Industrial area scan cameras utilize sensor technologies such as CMOS and CCD to capture images for various applications. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are known for lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and integrated processing capabilities, making them suitable for high-speed applications. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras excel in image quality and low-light performance, favored for applications requiring high sensitivity. Together, these technologies serve diverse industrial needs, including manufacturing quality control, robotics, and machine vision systems.</p></p>
