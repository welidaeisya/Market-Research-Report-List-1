<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chuck for LCD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are essential in the LCD manufacturing process, providing stable wafer handling. The Coulomb type uses electrostatic forces generated by voltage to hold the wafer securely, offering simplicity and high precision. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type combines electrostatic attraction with a mechanical support structure, enhancing grip and enabling smoother handling of larger wafers. Both types are critical for achieving high quality and efficiency in LCD production, catering to various manufacturing needs and technologies.</p></p>
