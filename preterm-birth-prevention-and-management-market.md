<p><p>Preterm birth prevention and management encompass various therapeutic approaches. Progesterone therapy supports uterine health to reduce the risk of early labor. Corticosteroid therapy enhances fetal lung maturity in high-risk pregnancies. Tocolytic therapy aims to delay contractions, giving the fetus more time to develop. Antihypertensive therapy manages maternal hypertension, which can lead to preterm labor. Magnesium sulfate therapy is used to prevent preterm labor and protect the fetal brain. Other therapies include various interventions targeting specific causes of preterm birth.</p></p>
