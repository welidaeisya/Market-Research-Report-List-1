<p><p>The steel billet market primarily comprises two types: slab billets and square billets. Slab billets are flat and semi-finished products used to produce plates, sheets, and strips, essential in construction and manufacturing. In contrast, square billets are square-shaped sections utilized primarily for rolling into bars, rods, and structural components. Both types serve as crucial inputs in the downstream production of various steel products, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, construction, and machinery manufacturing.</p></p>
