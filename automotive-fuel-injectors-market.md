<p><p>The automotive fuel injectors market is primarily segmented into gasoline direct injection (GDI) and diesel direct injection (DDI) systems. GDI technology enhances fuel efficiency and performance in gasoline engines by injecting fuel directly into the combustion chamber, allowing for precise control and improved combustion. Conversely, DDI systems inject diesel fuel at high pressure directly into the combustion chamber, optimizing efficiency and emissions in diesel engines. Both types play a crucial role in meeting stricter emissions standards and enhancing overall engine performance.</p></p>
