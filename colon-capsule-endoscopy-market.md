<p><p>The colon capsule endoscopy market is primarily segmented into two types based on photosensitive chip technology: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) photosensitive chips. CMOS chips are known for their lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and smaller size, making them advantageous for compact designs. In contrast, CCD chips offer superior image quality and sensitivity, which is beneficial for detailed visualization during procedures. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing diagnostic capabilities in non-invasive colon examinations.</p></p>
