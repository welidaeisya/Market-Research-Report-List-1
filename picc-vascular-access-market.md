<p><p>The PICC (Peripherally Inserted Central Catheter) vascular access market is primarily categorized into single lumen, double lumen, and multiple lumen devices. Single lumen PICCs are designed for straightforward intravenous access, providing a singular pathway for medication or fluid administration. Double lumen PICCs allow for simultaneous use of two distinct lumens, facilitating different therapies without interference. Multiple lumen catheters, which can have three or more lumens, cater to complex treatment regimens, enabling multiple medications to be delivered concurrently. These devices enhance treatment efficiency and patient care.</p></p>
