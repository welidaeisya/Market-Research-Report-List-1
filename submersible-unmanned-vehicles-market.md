<p><p>The Submersible Unmanned Vehicles market encompasses primarily two types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered, operated by a pilot from a surface vessel, and are used for real-time data collection and underwater tasks. In contrast, AUVs operate independently, executing pre-programmed missions and gathering data without real-time supervision. Both vehicle types are crucial in sectors like marine research, oil and gas, underwater exploration, and environmental monitoring, facilitating advancements in technology and operational efficiency.</p></p>
