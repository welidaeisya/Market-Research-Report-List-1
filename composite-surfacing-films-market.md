<p><strong>The Composite Surfacing Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave Processing</li><li>Out-Of-Autoclave Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite surfacing films are specialized materials used to enhance the surface properties of composite components. The market is divided into two main types: autoclave processing and out-of-autoclave processing. Autoclave processing involves curing composites under high temperature and pressure in an autoclave, resulting in superior surface quality and strength. In contrast, out-of-autoclave processing utilizes methods like vacuum bagging and room-temperature curing, offering greater flexibility and cost efficiency, making it suitable for various applications in aerospace, automotive, and sports industries.</p></p>
