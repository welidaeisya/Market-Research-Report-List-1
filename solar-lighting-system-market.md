<p><p>The solar lighting system market is primarily divided into off-grid and hybrid types. Off-grid systems operate independently from the main electrical grid, utilizing solar panels, batteries, and LED lights to provide illumination in remote areas. These are ideal for rural areas and locations without electricity access. Conversely, hybrid systems combine solar energy with traditional grid power, allowing for seamless operation during the day and reliance on the grid during cloudy weather or high-demand periods. Both options promote sustainability and energy independence.</p></p>
