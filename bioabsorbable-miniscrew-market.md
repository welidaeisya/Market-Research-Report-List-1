<p><p>The bioabsorbable miniscrew market primarily includes PLLA (Poly-L-Lactic Acid) and PGA (Polyglycolic Acid) miniscrews. PLLA miniscrews are favored for their mechanical strength and slow degradation rate, making them suitable for long-term orthopedic and dental applications. Conversely, PGA miniscrews degrade more rapidly, offering quick absorption and reducing the need for a second surgical intervention. Both types serve to stabilize bone structures while promoting healing and minimizing complications associated with permanent implants, appealing to various medical and dental professionals.</p></p>
