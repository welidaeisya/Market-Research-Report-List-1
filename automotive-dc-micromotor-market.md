<p><strong>The Automotive DC Micromotor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Brushed Motor"</li><li>"Brushless Motor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive DC micromotor market is primarily segmented into two types: brushed motors and brushless motors. Brushed motors utilize a commutator and brushes to transfer electrical energy, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for applications with lower power requirements. In contrast, brushless motors employ electronic controllers for energy transfer, resulting in higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced maintenance needs. Brushless motors are often preferred in advanced automotive systems, including electric vehicles and automation applications, due to their superior performance.</p></p>
