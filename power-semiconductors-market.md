<p><p>The power semiconductors market encompasses various components essential for managing electrical energy. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction, ensuring safe operation in circuits. Switches control the flow of electricity, enabling efficient power management in devices. Rectifiers convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC), crucial for power supply applications. Other components include transistors and thyristors, which support specialized functions in energy conversion and control. Together, these elements drive advancements in electronics, automotive, and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
