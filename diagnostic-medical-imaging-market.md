<p><p>The Diagnostic & Medical Imaging Market encompasses various technologies used for medical diagnostics. X-ray imaging systems utilize radiation to visualize internal structures. Computed Tomography (CT) scanners provide cross-sectional images for detailed analysis. Ultrasound systems use sound waves for real-time imaging, particularly in obstetrics. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) systems employ magnetic fields for high-resolution images of soft tissues. Nuclear imaging systems offer insights into physiological processes through radioisotope imaging. Other methods may include optical imaging and photoacoustic imaging technologies.</p></p>
