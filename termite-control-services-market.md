<p><p>The termite control services market is divided into two main types: organic and chemical. Organic termite control services focus on using natural and eco-friendly methods, such as beneficial nematodes, botanical insecticides, and physical barriers, to mitigate termite infestations without synthetic chemicals. In contrast, chemical termite control services utilize synthetic pesticides and insecticides, offering quick and effective solutions for severe infestations. Both approaches cater to diverse consumer preferences, balancing environmental concerns with the need for effective pest management. </p></p>
