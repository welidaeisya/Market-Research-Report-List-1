<p><strong>The Infrared Quartz Lamp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Infrared Quartz Lamp market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: Near Infrared (NIR), Medium Infrared (MIR), and Far Infrared (FIR). NIR lamps emit wavelengths between 0.75 to 1.5 micrometers, ideal for applications requiring quick heat transfer, like medical therapies. MIR, ranging from 1.5 to 5 micrometers, is commonly used in industrial heating and drying processes. FIR, with wavelengths above 5 micrometers, penetrates deeper into materials, making it suitable for therapeutic purposes, such as pain relief and muscle relaxation.</p></p>
