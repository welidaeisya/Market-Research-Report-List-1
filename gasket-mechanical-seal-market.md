<p><p>The gasket market is divided into two main types: metallic and non-metallic gaskets. Metallic gaskets, made from materials like steel or aluminum, are typically used in high-temperature and high-pressure applications due to their durability and resistance to extreme conditions. Non-metallic gaskets, manufactured from materials like rubber, PTFE, or fiberglass, are suited for applications requiring flexibility and corrosion resistance. Both types serve critical roles in preventing leaks and ensuring airtight seals in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
