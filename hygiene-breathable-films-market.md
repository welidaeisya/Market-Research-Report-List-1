<p><p>The Hygiene Breathable Films market encompasses two primary types: Micro-Porous and Non-Porous films. Micro-Porous Hygiene Breathable Films feature tiny pores that allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing liquid penetration, enhancing comfort in applications like diapers and medical products. In contrast, Non-Porous Hygiene Breathable Films act as a barrier to liquids but still facilitate airflow, making them suitable for protective apparel and wound dressings. Both types offer breathable protection, ensuring skin health and comfort in various hygiene applications.</p></p>
