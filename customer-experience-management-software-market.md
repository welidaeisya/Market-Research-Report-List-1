<p><p>Customer Experience Management (CEM) software is categorized into two primary market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based software is hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, as it operates on a subscription model. Businesses often choose between these options based on their specific needs, resources, and IT capabilities.</p></p>
