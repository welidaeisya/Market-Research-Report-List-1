<p><p>Airborne Collision Avoidance Systems (ACAS) enhance aircraft safety by preventing mid-air collisions. The market includes ACAS I and TCAS I, which provide basic alerts for potential threats. ACAS II and TCAS II offer advanced functionalities like guidance maneuvers. Portable Collision Avoidance Systems (PCAS) are compact, flexible devices for smaller aircraft, while FLARM is a specialized system for gliders and UAVs, using GPS and RF to detect nearby craft. Together, these systems improve situational awareness in aviation.</p></p>
