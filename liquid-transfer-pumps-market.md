<p><strong>The Liquid Transfer Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Transfer Pump</li><li>DC Transfer Pump</li><li>Hand Transfer Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid transfer pumps market is categorized into three main types: AC transfer pumps, DC transfer pumps, and hand transfer pumps. AC transfer pumps are powered by alternating current, making them suitable for high-capacity applications. DC transfer pumps run on direct current, offering portability and efficiency for smaller tasks, often used in vehicles or remote locations. Hand transfer pumps rely on manual operation, providing a simple, cost-effective solution for transferring liquids where electricity is unavailable or impractical.</p></p>
