<p><p>High-pressure misting pumps are essential for various applications, including cooling, humidification, and pest control. The market features two main types: pulley pumps and direct pumps. Pulley pumps utilize a system of belts and pulleys to convert motor power into high pressure, making them efficient for large-scale operations. Direct pumps, on the other hand, rely on a direct mechanical connection to generate pressure quickly, offering greater reliability and ease of maintenance for smaller systems. Both types cater to different needs and operational scales.</p></p>
