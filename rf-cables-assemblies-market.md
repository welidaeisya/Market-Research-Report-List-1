<p><ul><li>Semi-Rigid RF Cable Assemblies</li><li>Semi-Flexible RF Cable Assemblies</li><li>Flexible RF Cable Assemblies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF cable assemblies market comprises three main types: Semi-Rigid, Semi-Flexible, and Flexible RF cable assemblies. Semi-Rigid RF cable assemblies offer minimal signal loss and high durability, making them ideal for tight spaces and high-frequency applications. Semi-Flexible RF cable assemblies provide a balance between rigidity and flexibility, allowing for easier installation without sacrificing performance. Flexible RF cable assemblies are designed for maximum flexibility, enabling complex routing in various applications, though they may have slightly higher signal loss compared to the other types.</p></p>
