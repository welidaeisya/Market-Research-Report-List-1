<p><p>The pedicle screw market is primarily categorized into polyaxial and monoaxial types. Polyaxial screws allow multidirectional movement and are designed to accommodate varying alignment angles during spinal fusion, enhancing flexibility and stability. In contrast, monoaxial screws have a fixed axis of rotation, providing a more rigid connection, which is suitable for straightforward applications. Both types are crucial in orthopedic surgeries, ensuring proper spinal alignment and support while catering to different clinical needs and surgical preferences.</p></p>
