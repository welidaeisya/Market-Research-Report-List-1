<p><p>The ergonomic console market is divided into two main types: adjustable and non-adjustable consoles. Adjustable consoles allow users to customize height, angle, and position, promoting comfort and reducing strain during extended use. These are ideal for environments where multiple users operate the console. In contrast, non-adjustable consoles offer a fixed design, typically used in specific settings where consistent use by a single user is prevalent. Both types aim to enhance user experience and improve productivity while minimizing physical discomfort.</p></p>
