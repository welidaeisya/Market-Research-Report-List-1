<p><p>The eyewear anti-reflective coating market is primarily divided into two types: single layer and multi-layer coatings. Single layer coatings consist of a single film applied to the lens, offering basic reflection reduction and clarity improvement. In contrast, multi-layer coatings involve several thin films, enhancing light transmission and reducing glare more effectively. Multi-layer coatings are preferred for high-performance eyewear, providing enhanced durability, scratch resistance, and protection against smudges, thereby significantly improving visual comfort for users.</p></p>
