<p><p>The Glass Passivated Diode market comprises various types, primarily Fast Recovery Diodes and Ordinary Diodes. Fast Recovery Diodes are designed for high-speed switching applications, offering quick reverse recovery times, making them ideal for power electronics and high-frequency circuits. In contrast, Ordinary Diodes serve basic rectification purposes with slower recovery speeds. Both types utilize glass passivation to enhance performance and reliability, providing improved thermal stability and reduced leakage currents, thus catering to distinct applications in electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
