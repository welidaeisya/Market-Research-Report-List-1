<p><ul><li>Biomembrane</li><li>Synthetic Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-permeable films market is categorized into biomembranes and synthetic membranes. Biomembranes are derived from natural sources, often used in medical applications for drug delivery and tissue engineering due to their biocompatibility. Synthetic membranes, on the other hand, are engineered from polymers and are commonly utilized in water purification, food processing, and industrial applications. Both types facilitate selective permeability, allowing certain molecules to pass while blocking others, making them essential in various technological and medical fields.</p></p>
