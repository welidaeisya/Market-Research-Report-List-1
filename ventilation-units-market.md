<p><p>The ventilation units market is segmented into three main types: wall-mount, ceiling-mount, and cabinet-mount. Wall-mount units are installed on vertical surfaces, optimizing space while enhancing airflow in confined areas. Ceiling-mount units are designed for overhead installation, providing efficient air circulation in larger spaces, often found in commercial settings. Cabinet-mount ventilation units are compact and fitting for enclosed areas, commonly used in server rooms or equipment enclosures, ensuring adequate cooling without occupying excessive floor space.</p></p>
