<p><p>The industrial linear valves market encompasses various types, including gate valves and diaphragm valves. Gate valves are primarily used for on/off control, providing minimal resistance when fully open. Diaphragm valves, on the other hand, excel in applications requiring precise flow control, featuring a flexible diaphragm that seals off the flow path. Other valve types can include globe valves and pinch valves, each serving specific industrial needs. The selection of valve type depends on the application's requirements regarding flow regulation, pressure, and medium compatibility.</p></p>
