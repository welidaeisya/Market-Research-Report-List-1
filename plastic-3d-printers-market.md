<p><ul><li>FDM Thermoplastic</li><li>Polyjet Photopolymer Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic 3D printers market is primarily categorized into two types: Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and PolyJet. FDM printers use thermoplastic filaments that are melted and extruded layer by layer, ideal for functional prototypes and low-cost production. In contrast, PolyJet technology employs photopolymer resin, which is cured by UV light, enabling high-resolution prints with smooth finishes and complex geometries. While FDM is versatile and widely adopted, PolyJet offers superior detail, making it suitable for intricate designs in various industries.</p></p>
