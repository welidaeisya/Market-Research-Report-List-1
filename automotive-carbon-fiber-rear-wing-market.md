<p><p>The automotive carbon fiber rear wing market is segmented into three main types: folding, fixed, and split wings. Folding wings are designed to adjust their angle for optimal aerodynamic performance, enhancing downforce during high speeds while reducing drag during lower speeds. Fixed wings are permanently set in one position, providing consistent aerodynamic benefits, often preferred in racing applications. Split wings feature two distinct sections, allowing for tailored airflow management, improving stability and handling by optimizing downforce distribution on the vehicle.</p></p>
