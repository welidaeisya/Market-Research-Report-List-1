<p><p>Polypropylene peelable lidding films are designed for various sealing methods in packaging. The "Heat Sealing Type" uses heat to create strong seals, ideal for airtight closures. The "Cold Seal Type" employs pressure-sensitive adhesives to bond without heat, offering convenient application and quick sealing. Lastly, the "Self-Adhesive Type" utilizes inherent adhesive properties for easy adhesion, suitable for packaging that requires a simple peel-off feature. Each type serves distinct operational needs, enhancing product safety and consumer convenience.</p></p>
