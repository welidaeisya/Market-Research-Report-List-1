<p><strong>The Smartphone Camera Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated Sensor</li><li>Backside-illuminated Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone camera sensor market is primarily divided into front-illuminated (FI) and backside-illuminated (BSI) sensors. FI sensors capture light through the front, limiting their efficiency in low-light conditions. In contrast, BSI sensors are designed to collect light from the back, improving sensitivity and performance in challenging lighting, making them ideal for premium smartphones. As consumer demand for high-quality photography and videography increases, BSI sensors are gaining popularity for their superior image quality and low-light capabilities.</p></p>
