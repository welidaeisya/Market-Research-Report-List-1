<p><p>The fibre optics sensors market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Intensity Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors** measure changes in light intensity related to physical phenomena, such as temperature or pressure variations.</p><p>2. **Phase Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors** utilize phase shifts in light waves to detect minute changes in conditions, providing high sensitivity.</p><p>3. **Wavelength Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors** track shifts in light wavelength, enabling precise measurements of parameters like strain and temperature.</p><p>4. **Polarization Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors** analyze changes in light polarization to monitor environmental changes effectively.</p></p>
