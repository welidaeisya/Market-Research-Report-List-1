<p><strong>The Infrared Gas Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Path Infrared Gas Analyzers</li><li>Closed-Path Infrared Gas Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared gas analyzers market comprises two primary types: open-path and closed-path analyzers. Open-path infrared gas analyzers measure gas concentrations over a long distance, using infrared light that travels between two points, ideal for monitoring large areas. In contrast, closed-path infrared gas analyzers sample air from a defined volume, analyzing it within a sealed chamber. This type is well-suited for precise measurements in controlled environments, offering advantages in sensitivity and specificity for detecting various gases in diverse applications.</p></p>
